Security Advice

Based on the scan results, here are some recommendations to improve your website's security:

  • 1. Always sanitize and validate user inputs to prevent SQL Injection.
  • 2. Use Content Security Policy (CSP) headers to mitigate XSS attacks.
  • 3. Implement CSRF tokens in forms to protect against CSRF attacks.
  • 4. Disable unused services and close unnecessary ports.
  • 5. Regularly update your software and dependencies to the latest versions.
  • 6. Use HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.
  • 7. Restrict direct access to sensitive files and directories.
  • 8. Use parameterized queries or ORM to prevent SQL Injection.
  • 9. Regularly back up your data and have a disaster recovery plan.
  • 10. Conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
